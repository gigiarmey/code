What Is Safe Line?

Safe Line is a new app made for safety. Safe Line provides people with the help they need, when they need it. It will offer a quick emergency contact feature as well features that will help people find safe routes to take and watch areas of crime. Our goal is to prevent and lessen the chances of risks that people take when walking alone.
